About

Hello, my name is Dean. I have been drawing since I could remember!  My inspiration has been Takato Yamamoto (I have a piece tattooed on me) and many online artists that I have followed throughout the years. I have been taking all available art classes since high school, and I have picked up many mediums, from hands-on to digital. My life without drawing is composed of playing video games and reading manga or novels. 

My favorite medium is digital; I use my iPad as my tool. Although I would say my best work has been painting, Digital is more convenient and on the go, while traditional seems like a lot to tackle. My kitchen would be a mess for every project when I was taking my Elements of Design class! I have touched on character design by making my DnD character a fully fleshed-out character with a background and timeline. I also made a partner for him named Amon, making sure he had as complex a background as the first character. My biggest passion in life is to create. It doesn't necessarily mean only art. Humans create memories, love, and lives. Everyone is an artist one way or another, and I truly believe that from the bottom of my heart.
